About Me

Mikma

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a passionate and dedicated individual with a strong interest in finance and web development. My journey in finance has led me to explore various aspects of the field, and I am currently working on designing a finance website, which features a green logo symbolizing growth and prosperity. In addition to my work in finance, I am actively involved in community service. On February 17th, 2024, my team and I visited an animal shelter to understand their needs and provide support. We engaged various classes to inform them about our program, which resulted in positive responses and generous donations.
On the event day, we delivered food and cash donations to the shelter, managed the items efficiently, and performed for the shelter's staff and members.
We also helped design and print banners, and collected donations from family, relatives, and gym acquaintances. I am also interested in the Bajrangbali Savings and Loan Cooperative Society Ltd, an organization that reflects my commitment to finance and community development. My skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress, and React, which I use to create engaging and user-friendly websites. Through my portfolio, I aim to showcase my work and skills, and connect with like-minded individuals and potential collaborators.
